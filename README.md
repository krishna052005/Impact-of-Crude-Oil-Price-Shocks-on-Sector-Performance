# Impact of Crude Oil Price Shocks on Sector Performance

## Overview
This project analyzes how crude oil price fluctuations impact different sectors such as Airlines, FMCG, Paint, and Auto.

## Objectives
- Measure sector sensitivity to oil price changes
- Identify lag effects
- Generate business and investment insights

## Tools Used
- Excel (Data Cleaning, Correlation)
- Python (EDA, Visualization)
- Power BI (Dashboard)

## Key Insights
- Airlines show highest negative correlation with oil
- Auto sector shows delayed impact
- Paint sector demonstrates pricing power
- FMCG shows moderate impact

## Business Recommendations
- Fuel hedging strategies for airlines
- Demand planning for auto sector
- Cost optimization for FMCG
- Pricing strategies for paint sector
